\chapter{Preliminary work}

\label{sec:prestudy}
The objective of this chapter is to clarify the first phase of the project, which involved a lot of planning and research. In this chapter, the domain knowledge the team had to acquire before undertaking the project is discussed. Then, similar and existing solutions already available on the market is explored. Their advantages and drawbacks will be reviewed. Lastly, an overview of the different technologies used to support the development of the app is given.
